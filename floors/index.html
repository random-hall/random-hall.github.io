Bonfire

Come home, fair traveler, rest your feet. You must be tired from your journey (up four [ok, it's really three] flights of stairs). Have a rest whispering answers to the NYT crossword and spelling bee, pondering nonsensical philosophical questions, pondering sensical pragmatic questions, or listening to steampunk weirdcore complex vampire tunes on our beloved sound system.

Fear not, travelers with allergies, for we like our kitchen clean and certified nut (and many other allergens) free. Enjoy the cadence and cuisine of over 9.81 languages and cultures, and contribute your own!

We like to party in many ways. If you're feeling like venturing out into the world of ocean voyages, fairy carnivals, and reckless bears, participate in one of our several ongoing D&D or other TTRPG campaigns. If, instead, you wish to strut your stuff at a late-night gathering filled with late 2010's music, perhaps in costume on Halloween or not in costume on not Halloween, you will find a place here. We contain multitudes.

Come, friend, and flourish. For it's always morning on bonfire.
